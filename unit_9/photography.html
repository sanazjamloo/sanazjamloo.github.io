Insomnia

Exploring the notion of self-representation, Insomnia series illustrates the disruption in people's life. The main narrative is based upon my childhood experience of being exposed to the Iran-Iraq war for eight years and my memories of people whose life has been collapsed by the war. Insomnia project is a way to draw a mental map of the world of a forgotten and baffled Iranian generation; and to share the truth that I found it appalling. Images in Insomnia provoke no distinct future, nor a distinct past; they only represent a point of potentiality between the two. They embrace the traumatized immobility creating a dead calm that seems to stop the flow of the time.
